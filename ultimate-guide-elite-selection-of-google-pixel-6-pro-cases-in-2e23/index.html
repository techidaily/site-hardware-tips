The Pixel 6 Pro features a glass sandwich design, like many premium phones on the market. Any accidental fall or bump will do serious damage, which is why it becomes essential that you get a case to keep it safe and sound.

But since there is no one-size-fits-all case for smartphones, mobile accessory manufacturers release several options to choose from, each with a different level of protection, material, and design.

Related: What’s the Difference Between Phone Cases, Protectors, Skins, and Covers?

If you like to add top-notch and sturdy protection to your phone, ruggedized cases are typically the best choice. But if you want to keep the added bulk to a minimum, it’s better to go with a thin case.

Similarly, clear cases are ideal for showing off the phone’s design, and wallet cases help reduce the number of things you need to carry around with you.

For the Pixel 6 Pro specifically, there is one thing that you need to keep an eye on when case shopping. As the phone features a bezel-less screen with curved edges, you don’t want significantly raised side edges on your case as that will make it hard to use side swipe gestures. Most manufacturers have kept this in mind, so you’ll primarily find that the top and bottom edges are bigger than the side edges in Pixel 6 Pro cases.

Now with all that out of the way, it’s time to dive into our recommendations.

The recommendations below are only for the new Google Pixel 6 Pro, not for the standard Pixel 6. Make sure you are purchasing the right sized case for your smartphone!
