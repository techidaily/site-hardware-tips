## Discovering Excellence: A Comprehensive Review of 2024'S Finest Television Models

Whether you are a movie buff looking for a cinematic experience, a gamer demanding responsive gameplay, or someone needing a TV for general entertainment, there is a wide array of excellent TVs on the market.

## What to Look For in a TV in 2024

A TV purchase can be tricky given that there are many things to consider, from the TV display technology to its feature set. But you can make TV shopping easier by being aware of a few crucial details.

Display technology is one of the first things to consider when picking a new TV because it directly impacts the picture quality. Modern TVs use LED-backlit LCD or OLED screens . LED TVs are budget-friendly but have limitations in contrast and viewing angles. OLED offers superior picture quality but comes at a premium. High-end LED TVs with advancements like QLED and mini-LED bridge the performance gap, but often at OLED price points.

Beyond screen type, the color gamut , peak brightness , and image processing also affect a TV’s visual performance. Ideally, you want high peak brightness, a wide color gamut, and sound picture processing.

It’s equally important to ensure your TV has all the features you need, whether you want Dolby Vision HDR support or a high refresh rate for smooth gaming. Fortunately, most TVs cover the essential features, and only the advanced features vary from model to model.

Finally, factor in your budget and screen size. There are TVs for every budget, with value brands like Hisense, TCL, and Roku offering excellent options without breaking the bank. High-end features naturally come at a premium. Choose a screen size that complements your viewing distance. Larger screens are ideal for bigger rooms or those who sit farther away, while smaller screens work well for cozy spaces or near-field viewing.

We have separate detailed guides to help you choose the ideal TV screen size and provide a deeper dive into TV selection in general.

FAQ

Should I get a 4K TV?

Even though you can find some HD and full-HD TVs on the market, it’s best to spend on a 4K TV in 2024. 4K TVs deliver sharper images with finer details than lower-resolution TVs. If you’re concerned about the price, 4K TVs are cheaper than ever, and you can easily find affordable models. 4K content is also widely available via all major streaming services, 4K Blu-rays, and ATSC 3.0 over-the-air broadcasts.

### What's the difference between LED, OLED, and QLED?

LED, OLED, and QLED refer to the display technology in a TV. LED TVs are essentially LCD TVs using an LED backlight. QLED TVs are also a type of LED TV, but they also use quantum dots to deliver a broader range of colors than a regular LED TV. OLED TVs are, however, based on a completely different display tech that uses self-emissive pixels instead of a backlight to create an image on your screen. As a result, OLED TVs have far better contrast and inky blacks, whereas LED TVs have to rely on other technologies like local dimming to improve their black levels and contrast. However, OLED TVs are more expensive than most LED and QLED TVs.

Does the refresh rate matter for a TV?

A TV’s refresh rate plays an important part in the TV’s gaming capabilities to deliver a smooth gaming experience. Modern gaming consoles and PCs can output games at higher frame rates and you need a high refresh rate to make the most of it.

Refresh rate can also come in handy while compensating for content mastered in different frame rates. For example, a TV with a 120Hz refresh rate can easily display 24p content by multiplying each frame five times. However, a 60Hz TV has to rely on additional steps to ensure you don’t encounter judder.

What is HDR?

HDR, or high dynamic range , refers to a TV’s capability to display a broader range of colors, brighter whites, and deeper blacks. SDR, or standard dynamic range, can only display a fraction of the total colors a human eye can comprehend. This fraction of colors expands significantly in an HDR TV, which results in much more lifelike color performance. There are various HDR formats that content creators use to master their content. Not all TVs support all HDR formats. So, it’s vital to consider HDR compatibility while shopping to get the best out of the available HDR content.

How do I clean my TV screen?

You can clean your TV screen with a dry, lint-free microfiber cloth. Turn off the TV and use the microfiber cloth to remove dust and fingerprints. You can also dampen a part of the microfiber cloth with distilled water to remove stubborn smudges. Always avoid chemical-based cleaners, paper towels, and other abrasive materials to avoid damaging the screen.

### Can I set up a TV outdoors?

You can set up a TV outdoors, but you’ll need to pick a weatherproof model that can withstand outdoor elements such as rain, dust, extreme temperatures, and snow. Additionally, outdoor-rated TVs have better reflection and glare handling as you’ll have little to no ambient light control in an outdoor location. It’s also a good idea to install an outdoor TV in a covered area to provide extra protection.
