This cooler is compatible with Intel LGA: 1700/1150/1151/1155/1156/1200, and AMD: AM4/AM5 sockets.

 The Phantom Spirit 120 SE dissipates the heat away from your CPU with the aid of a solid copper base with a nickel-plated coating, with 7 copper heat pipes running from the base to the attached aluminum fin stacks. Dual towers combined with 2 x low-noise fans provide ample cooling whilst also keeping noise levels down.
