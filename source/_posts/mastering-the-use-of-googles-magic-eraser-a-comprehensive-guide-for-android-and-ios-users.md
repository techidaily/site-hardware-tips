##  How to Use Magic Eraser on Android

 The Magic Eraser tool used to only appear if Google Photos detected objects or people in the photo to erase, but now it can basically be used at will on most photos. You can choose to completely erase things, or more subtly "camouflage" them.

 First, open [Google Photos](https://www.anrdoezrs.net/links/3607085/type/dlg/sid/UUhtgUeUpU208046/https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.photos) and select a photo that has something you'd like to erase.

 Go to the "Tools" section in the bottom toolbar. You will not see this option if you aren't using a Google Pixel phone or a [Google One subscriber](https://android-location-track.techidaily.com/two-ways-to-track-my-boyfriends-nokia-g42-5g-without-him-knowing-drfone-by-drfone-virtual-android/).
