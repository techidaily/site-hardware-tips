##  What Is an "Unknown" Number?

 We're going to be showing you how to block calls from "unknown" numbers, but what does that actually mean? In short, it blocks any call from a number that does not appear on caller ID.

 This does not mean it will block calls from numbers not included in [your contacts](https://vp-tips.techidaily.com/new-simplified-guide-to-stories-mechanics/), as it [does on iPhone](https://ai-vdieo-software.techidaily.com/2024-approved-top-video-to-audio-converters-for-mobile-devices/). Unknown numbers are "unknown" because they don't display a number for caller ID. Calls from numbers you don't know---but still appear on caller ID---are not blocked.

##  How to Block Unknown Callers From Google Phone

 You can block unknown numbers in the "[Phone by Google](https://www.anrdoezrs.net/links/3607085/type/dlg/sid/UUhtgUeUpU207541/https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.dialer)" app from the "Blocked Numbers" section. You don't need a Google Pixel phone to use Phone by Google. Anyone can download the app from the Play Store. You'll be prompted to [set the app as your default dialer](https://screen-mirror.techidaily.com/in-2024-how-to-screen-mirroring-nokia-g42-5g-to-pc-drfone-by-drfone-android/).

 Open the Phone by Google app and tap the three-dot menu icon in the top-right corner.

![Tap the three-dot menu icon.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/2023-03-16_14-56-54.jpg) 

 Select "Settings" from the menu.

![Select "Settings."](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/2023-03-16_14-57-14.jpg) 

 Select "Blocked Numbers."

![Tap "Blocked Numbers."](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/2023-03-16_14-57-36.jpg)
