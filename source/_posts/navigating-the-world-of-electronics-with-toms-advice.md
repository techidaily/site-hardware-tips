 The monitor covers 125% of the sRGB color gamut, which seems decent. However, the maximum brightness figure of 300nits isn't that bright, so this monitor would be best used in a dim room. There are two 2W speakers built-in for[audio](https://tools.techidaily.com) output but a 3.5mm jack is provided for connecting external AV peripherals, as well.
