Never lose your charging cables or wall plugs again with the Permaplug charger lock. It's tug-proof, locks into place, and ensures your charger is always where you left it.

 This simple little gadget will ensure you never lose your charging brick or cables again. That all-important USB-C cable will _always_ be where you left it.

 With the PermaPlug, you replace the outlet faceplate with a custom-made cover that perfectly fits the included dual USB-A and USB-C fast charging wall plug, all while securing the cables in place. It's easy to install and only takes a matter of minutes. You've likely seen something similar in select hotel rooms, but now you can bring the same convenience and peace of mind to your home.

 Installation is a breeze, and both ports are certified as USB fast charging, so you know your iPhone, iPad, Galaxy, or Android device will recharge quickly. If there's one downside, you can only install it in one direction, and the cables will face up. While that's perfect for behind a couch or desk, it might look messy in the kitchen. And yes, it still leaves the second AC wall outlet open.

 As an added benefit, the tug-proof design prevents you from accidentally pulling the charger out of the wall when you roll over in bed to shut off the alarm. Additionally, if you already have a charging block and cable you want to secure, here's [another more affordable option](https://amazon.com/dp/B07W81LNTD?tag=htgprimedaysite-20).
